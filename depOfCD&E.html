Department of Conservative Dentistry and Endodontics
The Department of Conservative Dentistry and Endodontics is a vital component of our dental institution, dedicated to providing exceptional patient care, education, and research in the specialized fields of conservative dentistry and endodontics. Our department is committed to preserving natural tooth structure and promoting optimal oral health through minimally invasive and evidence-based treatments.

MISSION STATEMENT

Our mission is to deliver comprehensive, patient-centered care, while fostering a culture of innovation, excellence, and continuous learning. We strive to advance the art and science of conservative dentistry and endodontics through cutting-edge research, interdisciplinary collaboration, and community outreach.

SCOPE OF SERVICES

Our department offers a wide range of specialized services, including
  • Operative dentistry (filling, inlay, onlay and cast restorations)
  • Endodontic therapy (root canal treatment)
  • Dental trauma management
  • Tooth whitening and aesthetic dentistry
  • Smile design
  • Dental restorations (crowns, bridges)
  • Esthetic full coverage restorations
  • Microscopic and surgical endodontics
  • Management of dental anomalies and developmental defects

Education and Research

We offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs in conservative dentistry and endodontics, emphasizing hands-on training, didactic instruction, and research opportunities. Our department is actively engaged in innovative research, publishing scholarly articles and presentations at national conferences.

Clinical Excellence

Our state-of-the-art clinic is equipped with advanced technology, including digital radiography, surgical microscopes, and cone beam computed tomography (CBCT), ensuring precise diagnoses and efficient treatment.
