ORAL PATHOLOGY and MICROBIOLOGY

Department Of Oral And Maxillofacial Pathology of Maitri College Of Dentistry And Research Centre offers a pleothora of advanced diagnostic procedures and armamentarium; valuable both for the treatment of patients as well for the teaching of undergraduate and post graduate students. The department has both the light and compound microscope ; finest in quality for teaching the undergraduate students.
For the post graduate students the department has Penta-head microscopewhich is useful for group discussions between staff members and postgraduate students. The advanced research microscopewith specialized microscopy attachments (Fluroscent, Phase contrast & Polarizing) adds to the dimensions of research work conducted in the institute ; pertaining not only to the department but other dental departments as well. Add on this is the stereomicroscope used in various research program being conducted in the institute.
The department has fully functional histopathology as well as hematology lab; where patients are offered various diagnostic procedures at a very nominal price. The department has a team of well trained,skilled, experienced faculty members which expertise in the specialty of Oral and Maxillofacial Pathology.
