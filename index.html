About Maitri College of Dentistry and Research Centre

Maitri College of Dentistry and Research Centre, Anjora is run by Maitri Educational Society Registered vide no. 3383 dt. 03/02/2004

Permission for this college has been granted by the Central Govt. and it is affiliated to Pt. Deendayal Upadhyay Memorial Health Sciences and Ayush University of Chhattisgarh.
