DEPARTMENT of PERIODONTICS

The Periodontal Department is a specialized branch of dentistry focused on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of diseases affecting the periodontium, which encompasses the gums, periodontal ligaments, and bone supporting the teeth. Periodontists work to preserve the health, function, and aesthetics of the supporting tissues and structures surrounding the teeth.

Scope of Services

The Periodontal Department typically offers a range of services, including:
  1. Periodontal evaluations and risk assessments
  2. Non-surgical periodontal therapy (scaling and root planing)
  3. Surgical periodontal therapy (periodontal flap surgery, bone grafting)
  4. Regenerative procedures
  5. Dental implant placement and maintenance
  6. Crown lengthening and aesthetic procedures
  7. Periodontal plastic surgery (gum grafting, gum recession correction)
  8. Supportive periodontal care and maintenance therapy

Goals and Objectives

The primary goals of the Periodontal Department are:
  1. To promote periodontal health and prevent disease progression
  2. To restore function and aesthetics to compromised teeth and surrounding tissues
  3. To provide patient education on proper oral hygiene and preventive measures
  4. To offer personalized treatment plans addressing unique patient needs

Team

The Periodontal Department typically consists of:
  1. Periodontists (specialized dentists with advanced training in periodontics)
  2. Dental hygienists
  3. Registered dental assistants
  4. Administrative support staff

Education and Research

We offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs in conservative dentistry and endodontics, emphasizing hands-on training, didactic instruction, and research opportunities. Our department is actively engaged in innovative research, publishing scholarly articles and presentations at national conferences.

Clinical Excellence

Our state-of-the-art clinic is equipped with advanced technology, including digital radiography, surgical microscopes, and cone beam computed tomography (CBCT), ensuring precise diagnoses and efficient treatment.
